About Robotic Dub

The story of an independent electronic group

Our Story

Robotic Dub is an independent electronic music group founded by Don Titi and ManuManu. Since 2023, the group has been producing innovative electronic music under the Spacecraft Records label.

With over 111 tracks produced and 3 Legacy compilations, Robotic Dub has established itself as a major player in the French independent electronic scene.

Each release is carefully mastered by Alex Psaroudakis (The Work Shop) and enriched by remixes from Vince Michaelson, an essential collaborator of the project.
